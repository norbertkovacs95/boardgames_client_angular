Pick Pug Poop

Pick Pug Poop is not just a classic minesweeper game, since instead looking for hidden mines, you shall look for the hidden pug poops in the grass! You can choose from 3 difficulty, but be carefull, the harder the game gets, the more poop is left for you to collect.
